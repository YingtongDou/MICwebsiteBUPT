FAQ

International School, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications
May 10th, BEIJING, CHINA
Model International Conference, 2014
Technology and Humanity

What is MIC?

        Model International Conference (MIC) is a top student activities in Beijing which organized by IS, BUPT and open to all IS students. It gathered up academic experts from different schools and enable IS students to experience international standard conference process, in the meanwhile, understand the academic writing process.

How to understand the theme of this conference?

        Technology and humanity are precious treasure which human created in the evolution of human society. People develop technology and humanity and also benefit from the development of technology and humanity. Although they have different meaning and maybe have some contradiction, neither of them can be ignored. Attaching the same importance to technology and humanity, people can not only explore the secret of truth, but also reflect on the destiny and value of individuals. They are just like two wings of the society, taking human to a higher level of civilization. Through the theme—"Technology and Humanity", we want you to improve the humanistic quality and become a "complete" person.

Why do we have MIC?

        It is a good platform for students to communicate and exchange ideas. It provides students with a better understanding of our majors know more about the relationship of technology and humanity, and also create a blueprint of how to use the knowledge in the future.

How can I participate in MIC?

        MIC is open to all IS students. So you can choose your own favorite venue to listen to the lectures. What's more, if your paper has been entered, you need to do some preparations for proper presentation.

How can I know the conference schedule and venue issues?

        We will send you some important information of MIC to you by e-mail. Besides, you can also get the information from our official website or in your Technical Communication Skills class.

How can I submit my paper?

        Each participant is required to submit a paper in IEEE conference format, and an electronic version of the paper. The submitted paper is required to show in turn the paper title, submit date, affiliation (your class number), author's name, students' number and abstract at the top of the first page. Before 18th April, every class should select 5 excellent papers and submits them to paper sending mail buptmic2014@163.com. More questions about the paper: 2012213131@bupt.edu.cn.

How do I affect if my paper is Selected?

        Once your paper is selected, you will obtain the guidance from experts and teachers, and you can do in-depth exploration to your paper with them. You will also get some bonus in your Technical Communication Skills score.

What should I prepare if my paper is selected?

        Actually, we have specially-assigned person to inform you of the preparation and matters need attention.
