Committees

International School, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications
May 10th, BEIJING, CHINA
Model International Conference, 2014
Technology and Humanity

Human Resource Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The human resource department assists the minister to complete recruitment interview and pre-job training of officers. Officers of each department are assessed comprehensively and supervised by human resource department so that they can complete their tasks on time and with high quality. Human resource department also coordinates the relationships among officers and masters their status to make timely adjustment.


 Academic Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The function of academic department contains two main parts. One part is about conference papers. The academic department is responsible for collecting, classifying and filing MIC academic papers. Also, it gives supplement support by assisting the teachers to review and correct papers. The other part concerns the conference program. The academic department writes, edits and designs MIC conference program.
Secretariat
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The secretariat is responsible for contact, including inviting teachers from both International School and other schools, organizations and individuals in and outside of BUPT to participate in the conference. Another function of secretariat is arranging and handing out all the official conference materials, as well as sending and receiving all kinds of emails.


Information Technology Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The information technology department makes opening and closing ceremony videos and conference publicity video. The construction, management and maintenance of the MIC website are also its duties. It provides all the corresponding technological supports for the conference.
Marketing and Publicity Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The marketing department composes MIC 2014 Sponsorship planning and BUPT Requirements for rights and interests of MIC sponsors, as well as gets sponsorship for the conference. The publicity department designs the logo, posters, and publicity manual, writes press releases for 2014 MIC and does other related publicity works.


Finance and Audit Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The finance department completes financial regulations, financial report and cash flow chart. It is also in charge of the financial share distribution and account management. The audit department establishes the audit system and working regulation. It is also responsible for the conference financial audit.
Concierge Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The concierge department is responsible for greeting, guidance, advice and services for guests, as well as services for participants during the conference. The concierge department is also in charge of the award ceremony during opening and closing ceremony, and serves as auxiliary for pre-conference publicity preparation.
Auditorium Service Department
Reporter: AAA | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0
    The auditorium service department is responsible for the arrangement, layout and material preparation of MIC conference main and branch venue. During the conference, the auditorium service department arranges the venue and maintains the order.
