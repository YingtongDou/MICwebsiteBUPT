           Yuxiao LI

 

  

Yuxiao LI is the president of international school of Beijing university of posts and telecommunications, director of the Institute of Internet Governance and Law, director of the Beijing key interdisciplinary network governance, general leader of the “Internet governance and law” published books in national "five-year" key books. Peer expert of the ministry of science and technology international cooperation, member of network game review expert committee of the ministry of culture, adviser of China education network alliance, "special researcher" of state Internet information office, visiting scholar at the university of California, Berkeley, law school.

Pro. Li has long engaged in Internet governance and network law research, and has a profound and sensitive feeling to the development of Internet and the services to the society. Establishing an open platform for academic research, he also focuses on building the folk communication platform of network security between China and foreign countries and legal aspects at the same time. He has also conducted cooperation research with high-level foreign universities and research institutions. He has invited international famous experts and scholars to the communication and teaching in China, to enhance the level of China's Internet legal research, which played a positive role.

 

 

    Andy Watson

 

    

  

 

After leaving school, I gained practical experience in medical physics before graduating with honours in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, subsequently entering industry as a Control Engineer working mainly on automated ultrasonic inspection equipment and involving many different aspects of engineering such as customer liaison, design, installation and commissioning, operator training.

After several years, I decided to enter the education world. This was initially in technician education but I soon transferred to higher education, teaching communication engineering and control engineering. This additionally involved developing accredited degree programmes and programme leadership.

At the same time, I advised national qualifications authorities as a consultant on developing engineering education programmes and teaching materials, including technical education programmes in both China and Botswana.

As a Chartered Engineer and Fellow of the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), the work of professional engineering institutions has always been important to me. I have been active in IET networks, academic accreditation and professional registration matters for many years, serving on several national committees.

I have enjoyed all of my professional experiences, and believe that they complement each other and have allowed me to make a contribution.

I am a member of staff of Queen Mary University of London as a lecturer on the Joint Programmes with Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, allowing me to continue to contribute to this profession I enjoy so much.

 

 

 

 

       Matt Roberts

  

 

Matt Roberts is the Managing Director of the United States Information Technology Office (USITO), the leading policy-focused trade association representing the U.S. information and communications technology (ICT) industry in China. Prior to joining USITO in January 2011, Mr. Roberts served as General Manager of the launch team for the About.com China site (www.abang.com), and Chief Representative of Dow Jones in China, spearheading business development. Mr. Roberts has also served as an investment consultant, assisting multinational online media companies with China entry strategy and providing Chinese online media companies with strategic advisory services.
