About MIC

International School, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications
May 10th, BEIJING, CHINA
Model International Conference, 2014
Technology and Humanity

Model International Conference is an annual event organized by International School of BUPT as a part of the course of Professional Communication Skills.This event aims to provide to all students interested in academic writing and presentations an opportunity to have a close-to-real international academic conference experience as well as a rare chance to practise the organizational work of such events as the conference is entirely orchestrated by our students with advice from instructing teachers.Every year, we invite all students of this course to attend this mock conference where they can listen to selected student speakers to present their papers.
Scholars and researchers of related field are also invited to offer us their valuable insights. In the previous years, we have invited preeminent speakers such as BUPT ex-president, president Ye of IS BUPT and industry researchers like ..., along with research and teaching fellows of IS such as Andy Watson, etc.By now, the conference has been organized eight times. All students, teachers and invited guests have expressed their encouraging comments and support to this annual grand occasion, which is the lasting drive for us all involved to make it possible.
 

This yearly event would not be possible without the hard work of all our students and teachers, as well as the support from our sponsors and International School staff, for whom we'd like to give our deepest thanks!
