The Interview of the 9th MIC in BUPT Was Held Successfully
Reporter: Hu Yilong | Date: Apr 15th, 2014 | Comment: 0 | Class: News

 
        On the evening of March 20, 2014, all staffs who have went through two rounds of interviews came together in Addition 2 classroom to attend the first plenary conference of 9th MIC. In this conference, the vice presidents of each departments introduced the recent work plan in detail and the latest information and news about the 9th MIC was released out to all attendees.
        The beginning session of this plenary conference is about the introduction and the provisionally determined plan of the MIC. On the day of opening ceremony, the leaders and professors coming
from Queen Mary and BUPT will give speeches and all sophomores of international school are supposed to be present. Apart from the major session which is held in Big Fan classroom, there are six parallel sessions held in different classrooms which may contain different topics and themes to accommodate and cater to students of different tastes. After that, leaders of Human Resources Department, Academic Department, Secretary Department, Concierge Department, IT Department, Field Department, Financial Department and Market and Promotion Department in turn come to the stage to give detailed work planning and work introduction to the staffs. During this session, the emblem of 9th MIC conference was published and it was designed by the students coming from Department and Market and Promotion Department. In addition, the leader also introduced the appraisal regulations of this conference. Staff involved in the activity have to weekly hand in a form which contains the list of the tasks he or she has done in the past week and the reflections on his or her recent work. The final evaluation of the performances of the staffs is largely based on the references to these forms after the conference.
        In the ending of this conference, some tips and instructions for the future work were presented on the PPT, concerning the importance of information symmetry in the process of working, the proper methods of communications among the staffs and the cautions about the revision and decisions. These details may be unnoticeable in the daily work, but they are always essential and decisive factors that may determine whether the work is done excellently.
        The successful holding of this plenary conference gave a comprehensive and preliminary introduction to all staffs. And all attendees have got a better understanding of their own responsibilities and recent works. In this way, it is expected that everything will go under good and considerate arrangements with the efforts from all staffs and will further contribute to the ultimate success of 9th MIC conference in the near future.  << back
